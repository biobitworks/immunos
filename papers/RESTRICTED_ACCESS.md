## Access Policy

**Papers Archive download policy**:
- ✅ Download: CC-BY, CC-BY-NC, CC-BY-SA, CC0 (open access)
- ✅ Download: CC-BY-NC-ND (open access with restrictions)
- ❌ Do not download: Paywalled, TDM-only, subscription-only
