## üéØ Overview

Computational biology is an interdisciplinary field that applies mathematical and computational approaches to understand biological processes. It combines biology, computer science, mathematics, and statistics to analyze complex biological data and model biological systems.

## üî¨ Core Areas

### Bioinformatics
- **DNA/RNA sequence analysis**
- **Protein structure prediction**
- **Database development and management**
- **Algorithm development for biological problems**

### Systems Biology
- **Network analysis of biological systems**
- **Mathematical modeling of cellular processes**
- **Integration of multi-omics data**
- **Pathway analysis and regulation**

### Proteomics (Our Focus)
- **Protein expression analysis**
- **Post-translational modification studies**
- **Protein-protein interaction networks**
- **Functional annotation and pathway mapping**
