## üîç Research Gaps Identified

Based on literature review, current gaps include:

1. **Label-free, high-resolution ECM imaging**
   - Current methods require dyes or kill cells
   - No sub-100nm live cell ECM imaging exists

2. **Real-time ECM dynamics tracking**
   - Most studies are endpoint assays
   - Longitudinal live-cell tracking lacking

3. **Naked mole rat ECM characterization**
   - Limited published data on NMR ECM
   - Comparison to mouse/rat models needed

4. **AI-powered ECM image reconstruction**
   - Emerging field with few publications
   - Opportunity for novel contribution

5. **Mechanistic aging biomarkers**
   - ECM stiffening timing unclear
   - Early intervention windows unknown
