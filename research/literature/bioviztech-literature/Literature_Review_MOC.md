## üî¨ Research Themes

### 1. ECM Mechanics & Imaging
**Key Question**: How to measure ECM mechanical properties non-destructively?

**Relevant Papers**:
- Brillouin microscopy papers
- Phonon microscopy papers
- AFM vs optical methods comparisons

**Insights**:
- Picosecond ultrasonics offers best resolution + non-invasiveness
- Brillouin limited to ~1Œºm resolution
- AFM requires fixation (destructive)

### 2. ECM in Aging
**Key Question**: How does ECM change with age?

**Relevant Papers**:
- Aging journal papers
- Naked mole rat studies (if available)
- ECM remodeling papers

**Insights**:
- ECM stiffens with age
- Crosslinking increases
- Naked mole rats have unique ECM composition
