## ðŸ”¬ Research Questions

### Primary Questions (Phase 1)
1. **Can picosecond ultrasonic imaging resolve ECM at <100nm in live cells?**
   - Status: To be tested in Fast Grant
   - Hypothesis: Yes, based on materials science precedent

2. **How does naked mole rat ECM differ from mouse/human?**
   - Status: Literature review in progress
   - Hypothesis: Less crosslinking, maintained compliance with age

3. **When does ECM aging begin - before or after functional decline?**
   - Status: Unanswered - key question for biomarkers
   - Hypothesis: ECM changes precede functional changes

### Secondary Questions (Phase 2-3)
4. Can we detect ECM changes in response to interventions (senolytics, etc.)?
5. What are the early biomarkers of ECM aging?
6. Do ECM aging signatures predict healthspan?
