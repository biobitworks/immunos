- GoFundMe not anticipated (crisis emerged)

**Adjusted (October 2025):**

- Better application quality with extra month of preparation

**Decision 3: Bootstrap HealthClock until traction**

- Use GoFundMe + SSDI + contract work for Q4-Q1 cash flow
