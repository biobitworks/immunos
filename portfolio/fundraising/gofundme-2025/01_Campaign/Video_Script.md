# 90-Second Video Script

**Purpose:** Authentic, urgent video pitch for GoFundMe campaign
**Length:** 85-90 seconds (under 90 is ideal for viewer completion)
**Tone:** Direct, vulnerable, urgent but hopeful
**Setting:** Well-lit room, minimal background noise, face clearly visible

---

## üé• VIDEO SETUP TIPS

**Lighting:**
- Film near window (natural light) or in well-lit room
- Face should be evenly lit (no harsh shadows)
- Avoid backlighting (don't film with window behind you)

**Audio:**
- Quiet room (no traffic, roommates, HVAC noise)
- Speak clearly, moderate pace
- Phone mic is fine (no need for external mic)

**Framing:**
- Portrait (vertical) or landscape (horizontal) both work
- Your face fills most of frame (not too far away)
- Eye level or slightly above (not looking up at camera)
- Sit or stand - whatever feels natural

**Takes:**
- Film 2-3 takes
- First take is usually stiff - second/third better
- Don't aim for perfection - authenticity matters more
- If you mess up, just restart that section

## üé≠ PERFORMANCE TIPS

### What Works on GoFundMe Videos:

**DO:**
- ‚úÖ Make direct eye contact with camera (like talking to a friend)
- ‚úÖ Show genuine emotion (it's okay to be vulnerable)
- ‚úÖ Speak at conversational pace (not rushed)
- ‚úÖ Mention specific dollar amounts and deadline
- ‚úÖ Show your face clearly (no hats, sunglasses, dim lighting)
- ‚úÖ Be yourself (authenticity builds trust)

**DON'T:**
- ‚ùå Read from script on-screen (memorize key points instead)
- ‚ùå Over-rehearse (sounds robotic)
- ‚ùå Apologize excessively ("Sorry to ask for help...")
- ‚ùå Make it too long (lose viewers after 2 minutes)
- ‚ùå Use jargon (explain "FRO," "longevity," etc. simply)
- ‚ùå Forget the direct ask ("Can you donate $25?")

### Body Language:
- Sit up straight (shows confidence despite vulnerability)
- Use natural hand gestures (but not too much - keep hands in frame or still)
- Slight lean forward when making the ask (shows engagement)
- Genuine facial expressions (match emotion to words)

### Vocal Delivery:
- **Hook (0-10 sec):** Clear, direct, slightly urgent
- **Context (10-35 sec):** Storytelling pace, warm
- **Stakes (35-60 sec):** More emotional, vulnerable
- **Ask (60-85 sec):** Direct, hopeful, grateful

- Export at 1080p if possible (720p minimum)

## üì§ UPLOADING TO GOFUNDME

**Steps:**
1. Log into GoFundMe campaign editor
2. Click "Add Video"
3. Upload your video file (max 2GB)
4. Wait for processing (can take 5-15 minutes)
5. Preview to make sure it uploaded correctly
6. Position thumbnail at frame where your face is clearly visible

**Thumbnail tips:**
- GoFundMe auto-generates thumbnail from video
- You can adjust which frame is used
- Choose frame where you're making eye contact and face is well-lit
- Avoid mid-blink or awkward expression
