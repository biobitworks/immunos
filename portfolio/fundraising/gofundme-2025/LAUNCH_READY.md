## üí° PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### 1. Momentum is Everything
- First 48 hours determine campaign trajectory
- Early donors inspire later donors (social proof)
- Post your first donation screenshot (even if it's $10)
- "We're at $400 from 12 donors!" = encourages others to join

### 2. Thank Obsessively
- Thank every donor within 24 hours (personal message)
- Thank every share publicly
- Gratitude builds momentum and encourages more donations

### 3. Update Frequently
- Every 2-3 days minimum
- Show progress: "$1,200 raised! 48% to goal!"
- Create urgency: "72 hours left, need $800 more"
- Celebrate milestones: "We hit 50%! Thank you!"

### 4. Make it Easy
- Always include clickable link
- Mobile-friendly (most people donate on phones)
- Clear ask: "Can you donate $25?" (not "whatever you can")
- Offer share option: "Can't donate? Share helps just as much!"

### 5. Personal Beats Generic
- "Hey Sarah, you came to those dinners I cooked at Viva..." beats "Hi everyone"
- Mention specific memories
- Acknowledge relationship history
- People donate to people they know, not causes
