### When to Mention Direct Payment:

**Always include 3 options:**
1. GoFundMe (for those who want public campaign / receipts)
2. PayPal (for those who prefer direct / lower fees)
3. Cash App (for younger demographic / quick mobile)

**Format in messages:**
```

‚Ä¢ GoFundMe: [link]
```

### Benefits of Each Platform:

**GoFundMe:**
- ‚úÖ Social proof (others see donations)
- ‚úÖ Easy sharing (built-in viral features)
- ‚úÖ Campaign updates visible
- ‚ùå 2.9% + $0.30 fee
- ‚ùå 2-5 day transfer time

**PayPal:**
- ‚úÖ Instant transfer
- ‚úÖ Lower fees (2.9% for goods/services, 0% for friends/family)
- ‚úÖ Trusted platform (many people already have accounts)
- ‚ùå No social proof
- ‚ùå Requires email address

**Cash App:**
- ‚úÖ Instant transfer
- ‚úÖ 0% fees (for personal)
- ‚úÖ Popular with younger donors
- ‚úÖ Simple $cashtag
- ‚ùå No social proof
- ‚ùå Less common internationally
