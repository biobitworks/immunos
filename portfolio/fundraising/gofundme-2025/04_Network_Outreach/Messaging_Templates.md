## üìù MESSAGING BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ‚úÖ Personalize first line (use their name, reference how you know them)
- ‚úÖ Be specific about dollar amounts ($20-30, not "whatever you can")
- ‚úÖ Give them an "out" (share option if they can't donate)
- ‚úÖ Mention deadline (October 9 urgency)
- ‚úÖ Keep it under 200 words (people skim)
- ‚úÖ Make it easy (include link, don't make them search)

### DON'T:
- ‚ùå Copy/paste identical message to everyone (people can tell)
- ‚ùå Apologize excessively ("Sorry to bother you...")
- ‚ùå Guilt trip ("I helped you when...")
- ‚ùå Send multiple messages to same person in same day
- ‚ùå Message people at odd hours (respect time zones)
- ‚ùå Get defensive if they don't respond (assume best intentions)
