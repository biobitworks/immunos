## Critical Issues Identified (from Claude's review)

### ❌ Problems with This Draft:

1. **"Repay" language violates GoFundMe TOS** - Suggests quid-pro-quo transaction
2. **$900/$2,000 payment framing creates cognitive dissonance** - "Why fund someone who had $2,900 for events?"
3. **"42" theme may undermine crisis urgency** - Comes across as gimmicky during housing emergency
4. **November rent inclusion weakens immediate emergency narrative** - $1,800 for future costs dilutes "crisis by Oct 9"
5. **Missing hospitalization context** - Community knows about July cellulitis; builds credibility
6. **Passive voice and unclear agency** - "I received a free room but paid $900" is confusing
