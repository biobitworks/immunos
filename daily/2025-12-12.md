## Research Insights

### The UBE2V1 Study is a Game-Changer

**Prior to this study**: Hypothesis relied on correlational evidence
- Proteome waves exist (but many causes possible)
- ATTR kills supercentenarians (but could be age-dependent)
- Embryos fail (but chromosomal abnormalities explain most)

**UBE2V1 provides DIRECT CAUSAL evidence**:
- Maternal protein aggregation state → offspring outcome
- Reversible (reducing UBE2V1 → improved development)
- Age-dependent (UBE2V1 increases with maternal age)

**Transformation**: From "interesting speculation" to "testable model with direct evidence"

### The Cloning Data is Also Critical

**Original Prediction #2** logic:
- If aging factors are inherited cytoplasmically
- And cloning resets nuclear DNA but not cytoplasm
- Then clones should inherit full maternal aging load
- Therefore clones should age faster

**Nottingham Dollies falsified this** (clones aged normally)

**NEW insight is even stronger**:
- Cloning FAILS 95-99% of the time
- The barrier is epigenetic reprogramming (cytoplasmic state)
- The few survivors represent successful resets

**Conclusion**: Data supports cytoplasmic inheritance, but phenotype is "cloning failure" not "accelerated aging"
